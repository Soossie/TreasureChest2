Rules

Here you'll find everything you need to know to start your adventure in Treasure Chest 2.0. Discover the story behind the game, understand your goal and learn the essential rules and guidelines to help you navigate your journey. Whether you're a new player or a seasoned explorer, this page will give you all the information you need to succeed and find the hidden treasure. Happy adventuring!

Story

You receive a mysterious envelope containing a golden key and a treasure map. You are curious and want to find the treasure, so you start an adventure across Europe. You search for the right country and the right airport where the treasure is hidden. Along the way, you will encounter wise men and advice guys who can give you more money and motivation to continue your journey. Try to travel wisely â€“ if you run out of money before finding the treasure, your game is over.

The goal

The goal of the game is to find the treasure chest, which holds a reward. You must first find the correct country and then the correct airport where the treasure is located.

How to play

  1. Start the game by pressing the Start New Game button. You will receive money for buying plane tickets, the amount depends on the difficulty level.
  2. Find the correct country where the treasure is located. You can see the airport name, distance, ticket cost and CO2 consumption by pressing the buttons on the map. The colors of the buttons are explained in the box next to the map. Green buttons mean you have enough money to travel. If the button is dark green, you have already visited that airport. Red buttons mean you don't have enough money. A dark red button indicates you have already visited that airport.
  3. You can travel to your chosen airport by pressing the Fly button, which you find by clicking the map buttons. If you wish, you can get a hint to find the correct country.
  4. Each flight costs money and generates CO2 emissions. You can track your consumption in the Game Status table.
  5. You may encounter advice guys during your journey, who will give you motivational advice and money.
  6. When you find the correct country, your task is to find the airport within the country where the treasure is located. You proceed the same way as with international flights, but domestic flights are more expensive. Short flights are relatively more expensive than long flights.
  7. In the treasure country, you may encounter wise men. If you wish, you can answer a question to win money if you answer correctly. However, the wise men are greedy and will ask for money before giving you the question. If you answer wrong, you lose the money to the wise men.
  8. You win the game when you find the correct airport where the treasure is located. If you run out of money before finding the treasure, you lose.
